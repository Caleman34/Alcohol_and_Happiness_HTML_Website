Freedom of Life Choices

Analysis Figures











Happiness is much more than how much GDP a country has. A person's freedom of life choices also has a significant influence over their happiness. Countries with a greater perception of freedom of life choices have a higher happiness score. Sub-Saharan Africa and Western Europe have the lowest GDP, lowest freedom of life choices, and lowest happiness score. What is interesting is that Southeastern Asia has the highest perception of freedom of life choices, however they have are not at the top nor bottom of any metric analyzed.
